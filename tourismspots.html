Tourism Spot: Sada Pind

Sadda Pind is a Punjabi Culture Living Village Museum spread across 12 acres of land. It brings you a chance to experience authentic culture, colours and flavours of Punjab in one place. https://www.saddapind.co.in/ Situated in the holy city of Amritsar, just 8 KM from Golden Temple and 7 KM from Airport, Sadda Pind offers a perfect setting for a weekend getaway or a short stay with family and friends. The people behind Sadda Pind have over 29 years of experience in hospitality and administration. Sadda Pind is an endeavour to redefine punjabi culture and hospitality through commitment and world-class service. Sadda Pind offers something new for everyone. The young can reconnect with their roots and understand the traditions and values of their fathers and forefathers. The elderly can remember their happier times. Foreign tourists can sample the true Indian rustic lifestyle. Tourists from other states can experience the true Punjab in one place.

Timings : Village Timings:- 10:30 am to 10:00 pm Ticket Timings :- 10:30 am to 08:30 pm

Time needed: A day package allows you to spend 8 hours at Sadda Pind. It offers a complete tour of the place along with: fun filled activities, a guided tour, refreshments, an experience of the local arts and handicrafts, awesome shopping.

Entry fee: Adult :- Rs. 850/-
Children :- Rs. 700/- (3-4 FEET HEIGHT)
*Children :- (3 FEET HEIGHT COMPLIMENTARY)

Tourism Spot: The Amritsar Haveli

Experience the essence of Punjabi culture and hospitality at Amritsar Haveli. Nestled in the heart of Amritsar, India, this traditional haveli (mansion) offers a captivating glimpse into the rich heritage of the region. Step into a world where time slows down, and every corner resonates with history and warmth. Amritsar Haveli welcomes you with its intricately designed architecture adorned with vibrant colors, intricate frescoes, and exquisite detailing, reminiscent of a bygone era. As you enter, the aroma of authentic Punjabi cuisine tantalizes your senses, promising a culinary journey like no other. Indulge in a feast fit for royalty at the restaurant, where culinary artisans craft age-old recipes passed down through generations. From succulent tandoori delights to aromatic curries and decadent desserts, every dish is a masterpiece, prepared with love and authenticity. After satiating your palate, retreat to the serene courtyard or rooftop terrace, where the ambiance invites you to unwind amidst lush greenery and the soothing melody of traditional folk music. Immerse yourself in the tranquility of the surroundings as you sip on a refreshing drink or savor a cup of aromatic chai.

Timings : Village Timings:- 8:30 am–11 pm

Time needed: People typically spend 20 min to 1.5 hr here

Entry+Buffet fee: ₹200–600/Person

Tourism Spot: The Partition Museum

The Partition Museum, located in Amritsar, commemorates the tumultuous period of the Partition of India in 1947. It serves as a poignant reminder of the human cost and the profound impact of this historic event, which resulted in the division of British India into two independent nations, India and Pakistan. The museum provides a comprehensive and immersive experience, showcasing personal stories, artifacts, photographs, and documents that capture the harrowing journey of millions of people affected by the Partition. Through exhibitions, displays, and interactive installations, visitors gain insight into the social, political, and cultural upheaval of the time, as well as the resilience and the spirit of those who lived through it. As a symbol of remembrance and reconciliation, the Partition Museum fosters understanding, empathy, and dialogue about the Partition's legacy, fostering a commitment to peace, tolerance, and mutual respect among future generations. It stands as a testament to the shared history and the enduring bonds between communities affected by this momentous event.

Timings : Village Timings:- 10 am–6 pm
(Tues to Sun - Closed on Mondays)

Time needed: People typically spend 20 min to 1.5 hr here

Entry fee: You can purchase the tickets at the museum counter. The entry charge for foreigners is INR 250 per ticket, and for Indian residents, it is INR 10.

Tourism Spot: Sun City Amusement & Water Park

Suncity Waterpark in Amritsar offers a thrilling escape from the heat of Punjab's summers. Nestled amidst lush greenery, this waterpark provides an exhilarating experience for visitors of all ages. With a variety of slides, pools, and water attractions, Suncity promises endless fun and excitement for families, friends, and adventure seekers alike. From adrenaline-pumping water slides to relaxing lazy rivers, there's something for everyone at Suncity Waterpark. Guests can splash around in the wave pool, brave the twists and turns of high-speed slides, or simply unwind in the tranquil surroundings. For those seeking a break from the water, Suncity also offers additional amenities such as food courts, snack bars, and picnic areas, making it the perfect destination for a full day of fun and relaxation. Whether you're looking for an adrenaline rush or a leisurely day in the sun, Suncity Waterpark promises an unforgettable experience for visitors of all ages.

Timings : Village Timings:- 10 am–8 pm

Time needed: People typically spend 4 min to 5 hr here

Entry fee: Rs 600 per adult
